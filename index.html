About Me & My Mission

I'm a Biostatistics student at Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology, passionate about leveraging data to drive positive change in communities. My journey began with a fascination for numbers and computations, which evolved into a mission to make data accessible to everyone.

Through the Millennium Fellowship, I've been able to transform complex data into actionable insights that empower local businesses, schools, and individuals to make informed decisions.

My work focuses on bridging the gap between technical data analysis and real-world applications, ensuring that no one is left behind in our data-driven world.
